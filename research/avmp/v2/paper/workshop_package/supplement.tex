\section{Runtime Contract Boundary}

The current evidence resolves planner-stage replay, scheduler and KV-manager
structure, request-to-block assignment, worker tensor layout, and attention
block-table metadata. Mamba state-index and Mamba state tensor contracts remain
blocked. Consequently, controlled substitution remains future work.
